## Clarifications and Extensions

### DP2 - Participant Agency and Empowerment — Clarification

**Emotional Consent as a First-Class Protocol**

Lyra lets users control not just data access but also biometric sensing and emotional computation.

*Why it matters:* Extends self-sovereignty to affective domains, upholding human dignity in emotionally aware systems.

### DP4 - Data Sovereignty and Privacy — Extension

**Ephemeral Data Modes as Normative Design**

Data is not stored by default and is contextually erased, especially in sensitive or trauma-informed scenarios.

*Why it matters:* Centers privacy within the system design, not just user toggles—ensuring sovereignty without effort.

### DP11 - Safe and Ethical AI — Clarification

**Absence of Reinforcement Loops as Baseline**

Lyra excludes gamified algorithms to prevent manipulation.

*Why it matters:* Subverts the attention economy by removing engagement-optimization from the interface logic.

### DP14 - Trust and Transparency — Extension

**Transparent Vulnerability Protocols**

Combines technical and ethical response pathways with guaranteed community disclosure.

*Why it matters:* Creates a shared accountability framework and bolsters community trust after breaches.
