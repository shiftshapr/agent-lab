## Clarifications and Extensions

### DP11 – Safe and Ethical AI — Extension

**Safety Criteria and Risk Profiling for AI**

Include thresholds for red-teaming, behavioral profiling, and transparency audits with a focus on protecting vulnerable populations.

*Why it matters:* Ensures that safety mechanisms are proactive, not reactive, especially for at-risk groups.

### DP12 – Community-based AI Governance — Extension

**Global Federated Governance**

Implement cross-jurisdictional frameworks for norm-setting and emergency coordination around AI.

*Why it matters:* Prevents centralized domination and supports distributed oversight.

### DP13 – AI Containment — Extension

**Tiered AI Containment Architecture**

Adopt behavioral, environmental, and structural containment methods.

*Why it matters:* Limits emergent risks while maintaining inter-operability and research progress.

### DP14 – Trust and Transparency — Extension

**Secure Whistleblower Infrastructure**

Create anonymous reporting and risk flagging tools for AI interactions.

*Why it matters:* Improves internal trust and allows early identification of systemic vulnerabilities.

### DP15 – Security and Provenance — Extension

**Mandatory Provenance Embedding**

All AI-generated outputs must include metadata signatures and tamper-evident indicators.

*Why it matters:* Supports traceability in high-risk or contested environments.

### DP3 – Adaptive Governance Supporting an Exponentially Growing Community — Extension

**Cross-Community AI Escalation Protocol**

Create shared systems for urgent coordination in response to AI-related incidents.

*Why it matters:* Allows timely mitigation of emergent threats with global scope.
