This inaugural episode of *Bride of Charlie* presents a range of documentary artifacts examining Erica Kirk's background, including divorce filings, birth announcements, yearbook records, historical newspaper articles about her ancestors, and social media posts. The episode focuses on alleged inconsistencies between Erica's public narrative (raised by a single mother, date of birth, Swedish identity) and the documentary record.
