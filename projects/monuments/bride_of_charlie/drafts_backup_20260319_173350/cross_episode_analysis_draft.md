2. **Family Network Complexity**: Lori France's extensive corporate network includes entities connected to Richard Urbanbeck, who is connected to a documented $34 million fraud case. Multiple government contracts flow to her companies.

3. **Romania Connection Pattern**: 2010–2011 timeframe shows convergence of Tyler Boyer, Dennis France, Black Sea Rotational Force, and Nevada National Guard units in Romania — requiring coordination investigation.

4. **Pre-Charlie Relationship Evidence**: Documentary evidence (reality TV auditions, relationship documentation) contradicts Erica's implied narrative of limited dating history.

### 6.2 What Remains Unestablished

1. No evidence connects Erica Kirk to Charlie Kirk's death — the "assassination" framing remains a premise without documentation.

2. Paternity questions raised in Episode 1 were explicitly labeled speculation and lack artifact support.

3. Claims about mass shooting adjacencies require individual verification of specific proximity.

4. Government contract verification needed through official records.
