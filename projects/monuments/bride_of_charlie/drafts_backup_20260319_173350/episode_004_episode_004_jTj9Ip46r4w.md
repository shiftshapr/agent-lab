This episode presents documentary evidence including divorce filings and child support documents from 1995–2007 consistently listing Erica's birthday as November 22, 1988, rather than November 20. The episode also introduces an audio clip of Charlie Kirk stating Erica is "from the Lombardi family" and "direct line to the Lombardi family," alongside an obituary for Angelina Lombardo Abbis (1970). Additional artifacts include Instagram stories showing Erica with "Uncle Rick" Urban Beck at Universal Studios, kindergarten enrollment records from St. Ursula Villa (1994–1995), and podcast clips presenting conflicting timelines about Erica's Bible reading (2008 vs. 2016). The episode argues these documents and statements reveal inconsistencies in Erica's biographical claims and possible ties to organized crime figures.
