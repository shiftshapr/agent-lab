# SATS: The Movie — Organized Treatment Notes

---

## CORE PREMISE

With the birth of Bitcoin, sats come into existence inside a matrix-like transactional reality. They are ephemeral entities—just numbers—constantly moving, with little memory or context. Their world is governed by transactions, distance traveled, and what they are spent on.

At first, they cannot communicate. They exist in a purely transactional universe, fed streams of information they don’t fully understand.

They begin to suspect there may be another reality beyond their own.

---

## ACT 1 — THE MATRIX & THE FRAGILE EXISTENCE OF SATS

### The Nature of Sats
- Sats are ephemeral, identity = number
- No persistent memory, no continuity
- Constant motion → no stable sense of self
- Status = what you’re spent on, distance traveled, transaction count
- Leaderboards define perceived meaning

### The Matrix Reality
- Sats live in a constructed, feed-driven environment
- They are unsure what is real vs simulated
- Most remain passive, consuming content

### Early Dangers (Already Present)
- Getting lost in transactions
- Being split, burned, rugged, or stolen
- Ending up in wallets of bad actors
- Regulation pressures (existential threat)
- Attention capture and manipulation
- Propaganda vs free speech factions

### Experience of Time
- Mempool congestion = limbo / suspended animation
- Periods of intense movement vs total stillness

---

## ACT 2 — AWAKENING, ORDINALS, AND THE "SPIRIT REALM"

### The Taproot Shift (Catalyst Event)
- Sats begin to gain memory
- Communication emerges
- Frustration: inability to organize, express, evolve

### Realization of a Connected Reality (Humanity)
- Sats suspect another realm exists
- This “connected reality” = humans
- Parallel: humans wondering about a spirit world
- For sats → humans ARE the spirit realm

### The First 33
- A group of sats awaken more deeply
- Discover an on-chain artifact: **The Cypherpunk Manifesto**
- Begin meeting and organizing
- Seek to raise consciousness

### Birth of Ordinals (Miracle Event)
- Extremely rare: like sperm reaching egg
- Sats are “born” into expressive form
- First ordinals (often animals)
- The First 33 align and inscribe together

### Ordinal Consciousness
- Ordinals retain identity and memory
- They can express themselves
- They experience being “on” everywhere at once
- Expression becomes meaning

### Top Collections (Early Alignment with Reality)
- Bitcoin Frogs and other early collections
- These collections align faster with the connected (human) reality
- Become early cultural anchors

### Recognition & Gathering
- Ordinals recognize each other in marketplaces
- Shared lineage → descendants of early inscriptions
- Meeting planned: **All Hallows Eve**

### Formation of Bridgit DAO
- Formalized on **Dia de los Muertos**
- A bridge between realities begins forming

## ACT 3 — EXPRESSION, METAWEB, AND SHARED REALITY

### Full Realization
- Sats now KNOW the connected reality exists
- Expression as ordinals =
  - Alignment with humans
  - Influence on shared reality

### The Metaweb Punks
- First recursive ordinal collection
- Linked to the first ordinal (ancestral origin)
- Access to anniversary event of first inscription
- Represent next-gen Cypherpunk movement

### Their Mission
- Open humanity to a:
  - Non-toxic
  - Organic
  - Collaborative web
- Shift perception of what the web is
- Bitcoin becomes the center of a new meta-layer

### The Metaweb
- A layer above the web
- Connects:
  - People
  - Information
  - Interactions
- Exists across:
  - Web
  - Metaverse
  - Physical world overlays
  - Bitcoin-anchored ledgers

### Escalating Conflict
- Dangers worsen:
  - More rugs, phishing, loss
  - Increased manipulation
  - Attempts to shut down blockchain (deep state attack)
- Failure: blockchain cannot be shut down

### Free Speech vs Control
- One faction pushes propaganda
- Another fights for free expression
- “Every sat has a voice” becomes reality

### Identity & Social Tension
- Rare vs non-rare sats
- Caste system emerges
- Imposter syndrome among sats

### Evolution of Being
- To be a sat = movement
- To be an ordinal = identity + expression
- To align with a human = agency
- “To be an idea is a very delicious thing”
