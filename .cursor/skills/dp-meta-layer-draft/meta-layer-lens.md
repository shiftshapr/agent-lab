# Meta-layer lens (reference)

Use this as a **structured pass** over source material for any Desirable Property (DP). Do not treat it as a checklist to quote verbatim—apply it to sharpen claims and surface gaps.

## Six categories (context)

Properties sit under: **Authentication, agency & accountability** · **Sovereignty & privacy** · **Interoperability & experience** · **AI governance & safety** · **Security, transparency & trust** · **Community participation & feedback**.

## Lens questions (full pass)

**Identity, agency, accountability**  
Who is a participant? How is presence verified? What are they accountable for? How does governance scale without crushing agency?

**Sovereignty & namespaces**  
Who owns data and identifiers? What is portable vs. platform-trapped? How do namespaces stay anchored in user control?

**Interoperability & experience**  
What must work across tools and communities? Where is complexity hidden? What incentives align builders and users?

**AI governance & safety**  
When is the user interacting with AI? How is behavior bounded? How do communities govern models and automation?

**Security, transparency, trust, sustainability**  
What is auditable? What provenance exists? What is the honest roadmap—and how is the effort funded over time?

**Community & feedback**  
How do people improve the system? How is reputation earned? How is ownership distributed?

## Tone

Deliberate, structural, human-first. Concrete scenarios beat slogans. Acknowledge tradeoffs. No hype.
